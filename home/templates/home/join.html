How to Join

Firstly if you have a keen interest in magic and want to get more involved then you come to the right place!

Register hereand get access to our online shop where you can buy magic tricks to learn

If want to know when and where to find us, check out our calender for open events for everyone



Become a Member

To become a member firstly you need to attend some of our open events that everyone can attend

When you are ready you then will do an audition for your membership

After the audition boardmembers will make a decision, if a pass then you will become a member of the society able to attend all events



What is the Audition?

The audition is simply a 10 minute presentation/performance whereby you must perform a magic trick/tricks to a reasonable standard

It doesnt have to be overly complicated but also can not be a self working trick, a moderate standard of sleight of hand must be displayed

The audition is done in front of the club or if you would prefer to boardmembers only
